    Instructions:

    Constellations is a simulator of Conway's Game of Life. The Game of Life is based on two simple rules:

    1. A dead cell comes alive if exactly three of its neighbors are alive.

    2. A living cell dies unless exactly two or three of its neighbors are alive.

    Adjust gameplay using the controls at the top of the screen.

    Use the left side panel to add or remove cells.

    Hold down Alt and drag to pan.
